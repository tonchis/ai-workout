Day 1 – Squat + Push

Warmup

  • World's Greatest Stretch – 2×/side – 15–30 sec rest
  • Banded Lateral Walks – 2×15 steps/side – 15–30 sec rest
  • Bodyweight Goblet Squat – 2×10 – 15–30 sec rest
  • Wall Slides or Y-Raises – 2×10 – 15–30 sec rest
  • Front-Rack Position Hold (bar only) – 2×20 sec – 15–30 sec rest

Workout

  • Front Squat – Follow progression – 2–3 min rest – RPE 7–9
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 3×8 – 90–120 sec rest – RPE 7–8
  • Dumbbell Step-Ups (low box, slow) – 3×10/leg – 90–120 sec rest – RPE 7–8
  • Banded Face Pulls – 3×15 – 45–60 sec rest – RPE 6–7
  • Ab Wheel Rollouts – 3×8–10 – 60 sec rest – RPE 7–8

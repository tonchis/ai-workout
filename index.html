Day 1 – Squat + Push

Warmup

  • World's Greatest Stretch – 2×/side – 15–30 sec rest

  • Bodyweight Goblet Squat – 2×10 – 15–30 sec rest
  • Wall Slides or Y-Raises – 2×10 – 15–30 sec rest
